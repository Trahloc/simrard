# Simrard Open Mod Pledge

If your mod's manifest includes `simrard_policy: "Simrard-Open-Mod-1.0"`, you agree to the following.

1. **Public source.** You make this mod's source available at the URL given in the mod's `source_url` field. That URL must point to a place where anyone can view and obtain the source (e.g. a public repository).

2. **Unapproved mods.** Mods that do not provide public source and do not accept this pledge are not approved by Simrard. The official game build will not load them.

3. **Your work, your license.** This pledge does not change who owns your mod or what license you use for it. It only states what you agree to in exchange for your mod being loadable by the official Simrard build.

Builds compiled from source may omit the check that enforces this pledge. Only the official Simrard build is required to enforce it.
