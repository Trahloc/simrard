# Learning Room

The **Learning Room** in [Creatures 3](https://creatures.wiki/Creatures_3) is the area where [Norns](https://creatures.wiki/Norn) are taught words of the [Handish](https://creatures.wiki/Handish) language (as opposed to [bibble](https://creatures.wiki/Bibble)). It has one door to the [Norn Terrarium](https://creatures.wiki/Norn_Terrarium), which is the only entrance or exit. It contains a [cheese machine](https://creatures.wiki/Cheese_machine) and [toys](https://creatures.wiki/Toys) for feeding and occupying [baby](https://creatures.wiki/Life_stage) norns during the learning process.

The most important part of this [metaroom](https://creatures.wiki/Metaroom) is the learning machine, known in earlier games as the [computer](https://creatures.wiki/Computer). This has six buttons and a display on it:

The Learning Room, with the Learning Machine demonstrating the word "Like"- The red button is the power button, and must be on before the machine is used
- The left and right buttons will cycle to the previous or next word, respectively, and speak it to the norn - the norn will then try to repeat the word to show how well it has learnt it
- The button with the round arrow on it is the automatic function; turn it on, and the learning machine will cycle through the words, speaking them as it goes
- The two yellow buttons on the side switch between the two word sets, which don't seem to have any particular organization to them
