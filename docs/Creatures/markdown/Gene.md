# Gene

A *gene* is sequence of [D-DNA](https://creatures.wiki/D-DNA) that codes for a particular trait - eg the "[highlander](https://creatures.wiki/Highlander) gene" from [Creatures 1](https://creatures.wiki/Creatures_1), in which [creatures](https://creatures.wiki/Creatures) could metabolise [glucose](https://creatures.wiki/Glucose) to give a much higher proportion of [glycogen](https://creatures.wiki/Glycogen) than "[nornal](https://creatures.wiki/Nornal)", was a mutation in a gene controlling digestive function. Creatures' [genomes](https://creatures.wiki/Genomes), can be opened in external programs, which are used to [manipulate](https://creatures.wiki/Genetic_engineering) individual genes. Often a [hex editor](https://creatures.wiki/Hex_editor), or genetics editor with built-in hex support, is used for this purpose. "01 02 1C 00 00 03 03 3A 01 00 11 3B 01 23 38" is a hexadecimal representation of a gene affected with the aforementioned higlander mutation.
