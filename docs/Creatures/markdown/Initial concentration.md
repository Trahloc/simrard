# Initial concentration

The **Initial concentration** [genes](https://creatures.wiki/Gene) dictate how much of one [chemical](https://creatures.wiki/Chemical) is in a [creature](https://creatures.wiki/Creature)'s ["bloodstream"](https://creatures.wiki/Biochemistry) at birth. These can include:

- [glycogen](https://creatures.wiki/Glycogen)
- [glucose](https://creatures.wiki/Glucose)
- [boredom](https://creatures.wiki/Boredom)
- [ageing](https://creatures.wiki/Ageing) or [life](https://creatures.wiki/Life)
- [antibodies](https://creatures.wiki/Antibodies)

Common [mutations](https://creatures.wiki/Mutation) in these genes are [stillbirths](https://creatures.wiki/Stillbirth) caused by lack of glycogen, and 'infantile advancement' - where the [ageing](https://creatures.wiki/Ageing) or [life](https://creatures.wiki/Life) is at a level so that a norn is seemingly born as an [adult](https://creatures.wiki/Life_stage).
